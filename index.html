VIRAT KOHLI

Virat Kohli was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India. He began playing cricket at a young age and was one of the first players to train at the West Delhi Cricket Academy. Kohli’s early success led to his selection in the Delhi Under-15 team and later the Under-17 team. Kohli made his international debut in 2008 and quickly established himself as a consistent opener for India. He has played all three formats and has been captain of both India and RCB.

# ICC Cricketer of the Year (2017)

# Wisden Cricketer of the Year (2018)

# Padma Shri, India’s fourth-highest civilian honor (2018)

Virat Personal Life

Kohli's romantic association with Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma commenced in 2013. On 11 December 2017, the couple married in Florence, Italy. On 11 January 2021, they welcomed a daughter.
