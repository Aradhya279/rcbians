VIRAT KOHLI

Virat Kohli was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India. He began playing cricket at a young age and was one of the first players to train at the West Delhi Cricket Academy, established in 1998. Kohli’s early success led to his selection in the Delhi Under-15 team and later the Under-17 team, where he was the highest run-scorer in the Vijay Merchant Trophy.Kohli made his international debut in 2008 and quickly established himself as a consistent opener for India. He has played all three formats – Tests, One-Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 Internationals (T20Is) – and has been an integral part of the Indian team.Kohli has captained the Indian team in all three formats and has led them to several notable victories, including a Test series win in Australia. He has also been the captain of the Royal Challengers Bangalore in the IPL.
